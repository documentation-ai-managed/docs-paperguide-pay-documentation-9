## Overview

Create a new docs project in just a few steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to dashboard">
    Go to `https://docs.paperguide-pay.com` and log in.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Project'">
    Find and click the "New Project" button at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter project details">
    Fill in the project name and description, then save.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start adding docs">
    Open your new project and click "Add Page" to get started.
  </Step>
</Steps>
